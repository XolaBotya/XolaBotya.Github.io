About New Fields Ministries

Established in 2017 in Tsolo, Eastern Cape, South Africa, New Fields Ministries is a vibrant and passionate Seventh Day Adventist (SDA) ministry dedicated to spreading The Advent Message to rural communities. Our journey began with a vision to bring hope, love, and faith to those in need and to make a positive impact on the lives of people living in underserved areas.

Through heartfelt preaching and community projects, we have reached out and touched the lives of countless individuals, witnessing numerous souls baptized into the SDA Church. Guided by our unwavering faith and commitment, we aspire to be a beacon of light and an instrument of positive change in the communities we serve.

Our community projects encompass a wide range of initiatives, including Medical Missionary programs, Church Building Assistance, Food Parcels distribution, and Sustainable Gardening. These endeavors not only address the immediate needs of individuals but also aim to create sustainable and lasting improvements in their lives.

At New Fields Ministries, we take pride in being predominantly led by young Seventh Day Adventist youth who are driven by their passion for service and their dedication to the values of our faith. We firmly believe that the youth are the torchbearers of the future, and through their energy and commitment, we can create a world where hope flourishes, love endures, and compassion binds us all.

We extend an open invitation to all who share our vision and mission to join us on this transformative journey of faith and service. Together, let's work hand in hand to make a meaningful difference in the lives we touch and to spread the message of hope and love to the farthest corners of the world.

Donations

The Lord loves a cheerful giver. In 2 Corinthians 9:7, the Bible reminds us, "Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." As we embark on our mission to spread the message of hope and love, we humbly request your support through your generous donations.

Ellen G. White once said, "True generosity is not measured by what we give, but by what we keep. If we are willing to give of ourselves and our resources, we open the door for God's blessings to flow into our lives and the lives of others."

Your contributions will enable us to continue our ministry's work and make a positive impact on the lives of those in need. Together, let us sow the seeds of love, kindness, and compassion, and watch them grow into a bountiful harvest of blessings for all.

President's Message

Greetings, Beloved Brothers and Sisters

It fills my heart with joy and gratitude as I welcome you to New Fields Ministries. Our ministry is more than just a place; it's a family where love, faith, and hope thrive.

At New Fields Ministries, we firmly believe that we are living in a time of great significance. The messages proclaimed by the three angels in Revelation 14 are calling us to be steadfast in our faith and to share the good news with all nations.

Together, let us hold fast to the commandments of God and the faith of Jesus Christ, for in Him, we find our strength and salvation. As we journey in these last days, may the Spirit of the Lord guide our steps and embolden us to be His witnesses in a world hungry for truth and love.

"For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future." - Jeremiah 29:11

In the face of challenges and uncertainties, let this promise be our anchor, and let us be united in faith, hope, and love as we await the glorious return of our Savior, Jesus Christ.

In His Service,

Thembelani Yedwa

President, New Fields Ministries
